Hydroponics

Why is hydroponics the future of farming?



Domestic Supply

Hydroponic farming has enormous potential for production and delivery right within the UK, providing a continuous supply of fresh and healthy vegetables to domestic customers.


Year-Round Production

Hydroponic systems overcome climate limitations and enable year-round production of different crops under a Controlled Environment Agriculture (CEA) within the same farm.


Higher Productivity

The scale of hydroponic crops is larger than that of soil crops since nutrients are supplied above the ground, thereby preventing plant roots from growing into the soil.
